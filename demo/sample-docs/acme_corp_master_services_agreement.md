# Master Services Agreement

**Between**: BetterDesign Inc. ("Provider") and Acme Corp ("Client")

**Effective Date**: April 1, 2025

**Agreement Number**: MSA-2025-0042

---

## 1. Services

Provider shall deliver the following services to Client:

- UX/UI design for Acme Corp's enterprise dashboard product ("AcmeDash")
- Design system creation and maintenance
- Monthly design reviews and iteration cycles
- Quarterly usability testing reports

## 2. Term

This Agreement is effective for an initial term of **12 months** from the Effective Date (April 1, 2025 through March 31, 2026), with automatic renewal for successive 12-month periods unless either party provides 60 days' written notice of non-renewal.

## 3. Compensation

- **Monthly retainer**: $15,000/month ($180,000 annually)
- **Payment terms**: Net 30 from invoice date
- **Rate increase**: Annual rate adjustment capped at 5%, effective upon renewal

## 5. Scope of Work — Phase 1

### AcmeDash Enterprise Redesign
- **Deliverables**: Wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, interactive prototypes, design system components
- **Timeline**: Phase 1 completion by August 2025
- **Status**: Phase 1 completed on schedule. Phase 2 (mobile responsive) kicked off September 2025.

## 6. Performance Metrics

- Monthly design delivery rate: 95%+ on-time
- Usability test score improvement target: 20% lift in task completion rate
- Client satisfaction score: quarterly NPS survey, target 8+

## 7. Intellectual Property

All deliverables created under this Agreement shall be owned by Client upon full payment. Provider retains rights to general design methodologies and non-client-specific tools developed during engagement.

## 8. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information for 3 years following termination. This includes product roadmaps, financial data, user research findings, and competitive analysis shared during the engagement.

## 9. Termination

Either party may terminate with 60 days' written notice. Client shall pay for all services rendered through the termination date, plus any pre-approved work in progress.
