# Meeting Briefing Format

Use this structure for all meeting prep briefings:

---

## 📋 Meeting: {meeting_title}

**When**: {day}, {date} at {time} ({duration})
**With**: {attendee_names_and_roles}

---

### 🧠 What You Know (Memory)

{If Redis memory returned results, list them as bullets. Lead with the most actionable: open action items first, then past decisions, then relationship context.}

- **Last meeting**: {date} — {summary}
- **Open action item**: {description} (due {deadline})
- **Note**: {person} prefers {preference}

{If no memory exists: "No prior meeting history. After this meeting, debrief with me to start building context."}

---

### 📧 Recent Communications

{Summarize key topics from Gmail threads with this person. Group by thread subject.}

- **Re: {subject}** ({date}) — {1-line summary}
- **Re: {subject}** ({date}) — {1-line summary}

{If no emails found: "No recent email threads found."}

---

### 🌐 External Research

**LinkedIn**
- {person}: {headline} — {bio_summary} ([profile]({linkedin_url}))
- {company}: {industry}, ~{employeeCount} employees — {description_summary}

**Twitter/X**
- Recent: "{tweet_text}" — @{author}, {date}

**Crunchbase**
- {company}: {funding_total} raised ({last_funding_type}). Investors: {investor_list}

**Google News**
- {news_headline} — [source]({url})

**Company Website**
- From {company} About page: {key_excerpt}

{If a scraper returned no data, omit that subsection. If all scrapers failed: "External research unavailable."}

---

### 📄 Relevant Documents

{Excerpts from Contextual AI document search. Cite the document name.}

- From **{doc_name}**: "{relevant_excerpt}"

{If no documents found: "No relevant documents in your library."}

---

### 💡 Suggested Talking Points

{Synthesize 3-5 talking points from ALL sources above. These should be actionable and specific.}

1. {talking_point — grounded in a specific source}
2. {talking_point}
3. {talking_point}

---

### ⚡ Open Items to Address

{Any action items from memory or emails that need follow-up in this meeting.}

- [ ] {action_item}
- [ ] {action_item}
